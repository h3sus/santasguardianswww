Yes, we train fighter elves. Why?

Because, when the world was young, trolls invaded Santa's realm, Lapland. The best and bravest of Santa's elves were sent against them. They were victorious, and began to be known as Santa's Guardians.

Now, centuries later, trolls have been sighted in Santa's hometown Rovaniemi again, but the elves are ready to train more Guardians for Santa.

All they need is fresh recruits!

Location

We are located in the heart of Santa's base of operations, very close to SantaPark! And not too far from Santa's Village at the Arctic Circle, either.

10 minutes before your booked session begins, we will come and escort you up from one of two pickup spots:

  1. SantaPark bus stop, or
  2. Hostel Ibedcity yard.

It is a 5 minute walk from both. Just select your pickup spot while booking.

It is best leave to us your phone number when booking, so that we can call you if we don't see you there!
